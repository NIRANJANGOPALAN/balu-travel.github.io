About Balu Travel

Balu Travel is a family-run business offering personalized service and competitive prices on flights all over the world with major airlines such as British Airways, Virgin Atlantic, American Airlines, KLM, Air France, Emirates and more. We often bulk buy seats on popular flights so that we can offer you the most competitive rates in the business.

Wherever you want to go, Balu Travel can find the right solution to meet your needs. We've been in the business since the 1960s and have built up a huge network of contacts over that time. This means we have access to the best deals on the market and we can pass on the savings to you.

At Balu Travel, we provide high-quality travel solutions at exceptional value for money. We serve customers internationally, so contact us for a quality service and we'll find you the best price on flights, wherever you want to go.

Our knowledgeable and experienced team will be on hand to provide you with the first-class service that has become our calling card.
