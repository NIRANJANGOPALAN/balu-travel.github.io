About Balu Travel

We book best seats on popular flights and we can offer you the most competitive rates in the business.

Wherever you want to go, Balu Travel can find the right solution to meet your needs. With six decades of experience in the industry since our founding in 1964, we've built an extensive network of contacts that gives us access to the best deals in the market. Our longstanding partnerships with all major airlines allow us to offer a wide range of flight options, from economy, premium economy to business and first class travel.

At Balu Travel, we provide high-quality travel solutions at exceptional value for money. We see cusgomers as family, so contact us for a high quality service and we'll find you the best price for your next holiday, wherever you wish to travel.

A Few Words About Us

1964

Our founders, late Mr Moti Lal Balu and late Mrs Nasib Kaur Balu started a small butcher and grocery business shop located in the then Elite Cinema called "Balu & Co" on Soho Road, Birmingham, catering to a diverse community. Where we served a wide range of customers, including people from India, Pakistan, Jamaica reflecting the multicultural fabric of the area. Balu's commitment to quality and community engagement has established this shop as a local favorite, contributing to the vibrant atmosphere of Soho Road.

1968

Balu Travel was established by the late Mr Manohar Lal Balu, Mr and Mrs Balu's eldest son, in 1968, marking a significant expansion of the family business. This venture catered to the growing demand for travel services among the local immigrant population, enabling many to reconnect with their roots. Over the years, Balu Travel has evolved, offering diverse travel options and adapting to the changing needs of the clientele it serves.

2006

In 2006, we completely restructured and renovated our building known today as "Balu-Elite House", creating a modern space designed to better serve our clients and enhance their overall experience. This transformation included state-of-the-art facilities, improved accessibility, large private car parking and a welcoming atmosphere that reflects our commitment to quality service. The updated environment has allowed us to showcase our products more effectively and provide an inviting space for our valued customers.

Today

Today, Bob Balu serves as Director in Balu Travel and co-partner with his brother Toby Balu of the parent company "The Balu Partnership", where he oversees operations and financial strategies. The company specialises in offering competitive flight arrangements, ensuring clients receive the best rates and services. With the primary focus on customer satisfaction, Balu Travel has built a reputation for reliability and value, catering to a diverse clientele that reflects the multicultural community we live in. Bob's leadership continues to drive the company’s growth and adaptability in the evolving travel industry
