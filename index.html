About Balu Travel

We book best seats on popular flights and we can offer you the most competitive rates in the business.

A Few Words About Us

1964

Moti Lal Balu started a small butchers and grocery shop named "Balu & Co" on Soho Road in Handsworth, catering to a diverse community. His business serves a wide range of customers, including people from India, Pakistan, Jamaica reflecting the multicultural fabric of the area. Balu's commitment to quality and community engagement has established his shop as a local favorite, contributing to the vibrant atmosphere of Soho Road.

1968

Balu Travel was established by Manohar Lal Balu, Mr. Balu's eldest son, in 1968, marking a significant expansion of the family business. This venture catered to the growing demand for travel services among the local immigrant population, enabling many to reconnect with their roots. Over the years, Balu Travel has evolved, offering diverse travel options and adapting to the changing needs of the community it serves.

2006

In 2006, we completely restructured and renovated our building, creating a modern space designed to better serve our clients and enhance their overall experience. This transformation included state-of-the-art facilities, improved accessibility, big private car parking and a welcoming atmosphere that reflects our commitment to quality service. The updated environment has allowed us to showcase our products more effectively and provide an inviting space for our valued customers.

Today

Today, Bob Balu serves as the head of group finance and partner in Balu Travel, where he oversees operations and financial strategies. The company specializes in offering competitive flight arrangements, ensuring clients receive the best rates and services. With a focus on customer satisfaction, Balu Travel has built a reputation for reliability and value, catering to a diverse clientele that reflects the multicultural community of Handsworth. Bob's leadership continues to drive the company’s growth and adaptability in the evolving travel industry
